About us

Behind Laika is a team of young women, Veronika, Alex, and Sophia, who have a passion for coding, robotics, programming, and helping the community. Laika initially started as a final project for the JP Morgan-Chase Girls Who Code program.
Laika strives to intersect techonology and accessibility, and break barriers within technology and beyond.
