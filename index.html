About us

Behind Laika is a team of young women: Veronika, Alex and Sophia; who have a passion for coding, robotics, programming, and helping the community. Laika initially started as a final project for the JP Morgan-Chase Girls Who Code program. Laika strives to intersect techonology with accessibility and break barriers within technology and beyond.

About Laika

Laika is a revolutionary robotic service dog designed for people who suffer from anxiety and PTSD and cannot afford a regular service dog. She can cuddle, dispense medication, call an emergency contact, and be pet. She is the first of her kind and strives to enrich lives through uniting technology and healthcare.

What can Laika do?

We are currently working on basic prototypes.

Mission

We work on Laika, not for us, but for those who cannot obtain a real service dog. Laika is for those who need that help. We are simply here to provide it.
